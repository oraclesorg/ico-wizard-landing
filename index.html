Sale process

Coral offers the general public a simple interface to buy your tokens, and offers you a complete dashboard to keep track of the sale's progress and statistics.

If needed, Coral is also able to guide individuals through a simple process for creating a unique, standard wallet where they can store their tokens.

Pre-sale process

Tokens are usually offered privately before the public sale begins. Use Coral to allow early participants to purchase tokens before opening the sale to the general public.

Funds are automatically sent to the token sale smart contract before the opening date.
